Not Legos.

Vex Robotics offers complex games that incorporate engineering, programming, and most importantly collaboration. In teams, students compete against each other to see who can score the most points in each year's respective game. With limited resources, motors, and time, Vex offers a great challenge to any student willing.

Not Just a Club, But a family

The SwampBots are more than just a robotics team; they're a close-knit group of students who share a passion for problem-solving and innovation. They work together, combining individual strengths to tackle challenges and achieve their goals. This sense of community drives their success, creating an environment where every member is valued and supported. In the end, it's not just about winning competitions, but about growing and learning together as a team.
